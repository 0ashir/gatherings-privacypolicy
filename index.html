Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 26, 2024

At Gatherings, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our app, where organizers can post and sell tickets for events like concerts, seminars, and more.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information from users to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information:

    Picture, Name, Email, Address, Interests.

  • Payment Information:

    For event ticket purchases and money transfers, we use third-party services like Stripe and PayPal, which may require your credit card information. We do not store this information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • Personalizing your experience within the app.
  • Facilitating ticket purchases and event participation.

3. Third Party Services

We integrate the following third-party services to facilitate payments and transactions:

  • Stripe: To process credit card payments.
  • Paypal: To process payments and transfers.
  • CoinPayments: For cryptocurrency transactions.
These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your personal and financial information. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

4. User Rights

You have the right to.

  • Access and Update: View or update your personal information within the app.
  • Delete Your Data: Request the deletion of your data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us at gatheringspk@gmail.com.

5. Cookies or Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies in the app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties. The only time your data is used by third parties is for payment processing through Stripe, PayPal , and CoinPayments.

7. Security Measures

We prioritize the protection of your data. Your credit card and financial information are securely processed by our third-party providers (Stripe and PayPal), and we do not store any payment details on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete the data.

9. Global Reach

Our app is available globally and is not limited to specific regions. However, users are responsible for ensuring that they comply with any applicable data privacy regulations in their jurisdiction.

10. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. If any significant changes are made, we will notify you via email.

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us at:
Email: gatheringspk@gmail.com.
